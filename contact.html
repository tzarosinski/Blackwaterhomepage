Quick Answers to Common Questions

How long does septic installation take?

Typically 1-2 weeks from permit approval to final inspection, depending on system type and weather. We'll give you a specific timeline with your quote.

Do you handle permits?

Yes. We handle all permit applications and coordinate inspections as part of our total site work service.

Can you give me a ballpark price over the phone?

We're happy to discuss general pricing ranges, but accurate quotes require seeing your property. Every site is different - soil conditions, access, size, and scope all affect pricing.

Do you offer payment plans?

We work with customers on payment schedules. Typically we require a deposit to start, with remaining balance due at milestones or completion.

How far do you travel?

We serve an 80-mile radius from Tyler, covering eight East Texas counties. If you're on the edge of our service area, contact us - we may still be able to help.

What if I only need one service?

No problem. We handle individual services and complete packages. Whether you need just a septic system or complete total site work, we're here to help.

Still Have Questions?

We're happy to discuss your project with no obligation. Whether you're ready to start tomorrow or just planning for next year, we're here to provide honest information and accurate quotes.
