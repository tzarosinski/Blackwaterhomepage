Who We Are At Blackwater Land Management

Total Site Work Experts Serving East Texas

Our Story

Blackwater Land Management was built on a simple idea: property owners shouldn't have to coordinate five different contractors just to prepare their land. We saw customers frustrated by juggling septic companies, clearing contractors, driveway builders, and utility coordination - all on different schedules with different communication styles.

So we built a better solution. One company that handles complete total site work from septic installation through final grading. One team that understands how each phase affects the next. One point of contact from your first call to project completion.

Today, we're proud to be the preferred site work contractor for mobile home dealers and trusted by property owners, builders, and developers across eight East Texas counties.

What Makes Blackwater Different

Complete Total Site Work Capability

Most contractors specialize in one thing. Septic companies do septic. Clearing companies clear land. You're left coordinating schedules and hoping everyone shows up.

We do it all. Septic, clearing, pads, driveways, culverts, utilities - everything needed to go from raw land to build-ready. And we coordinate it as one project, not separate jobs.

East Texas Expertise

We don't just work in East Texas - we're experts in East Texas conditions. We know the red clay soils of Smith County. We know the sandy areas in parts of Cherokee County. We know which counties require what permits and how local inspectors operate.

This knowledge matters. It means your septic system is designed right the first time. Your house pad drains properly in heavy rain. Your driveway is built to handle our weather.

Partnership Approach

We don't treat projects as transactions. We build relationships. That's why mobile home dealers trust us with their customers' sites. That's why we get referrals from realtors, builders, and past customers.

You'll have our direct number. We'll communicate throughout the project. If something changes or a question comes up, you call us - not a dispatch center or answering service.
