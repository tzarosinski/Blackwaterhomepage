Frequently Asked Questions About Total Site Work in East Texas

Everything you need to know about septic installation, land clearing, and site preparation

What's included in land clearing and brush removal?

Our land clearing service includes tree removal, stump grinding, brush clearing, and site leveling. We can handle small residential lots or large acreage projects. You get a clean, build-ready site that's properly graded for drainage.

Do I need a permit for a driveway in Smith County?

Yes, most East Texas counties require permits for driveways that connect to public roads, especially if a culvert is needed. We handle all driveway permits and culvert sizing requirements for Smith, Cherokee, Henderson, Gregg, Rusk, Van Zandt, Wood, and Anderson counties. Each county has different requirements for culvert size and driveway width - we know them all.

Can you handle everything for a new build or mobile home site?

Absolutely! That's what "total site work" means. We coordinate septic installation, land clearing, house pad development, driveway construction, culvert installation, utility trenching, and electrical hookups. You work with one contractor instead of juggling 5-6 different companies. We're trusted by mobile home dealers throughout East Texas because we make the process simple and keep projects on schedule.

What areas do you serve in East Texas?

We serve Tyler, TX and an 80-mile radius, including Smith County, Cherokee County, Henderson County, Gregg County, Rusk County, Van Zandt County, Wood County, and Anderson County. Not sure if we serve your area? Give us a call at 903-497-7459 - we might be able to help or refer you to a trusted contractor.

What makes East Texas soil different for septic and site work?

East Texas has unique clay-heavy soils that require special expertise for septic systems and proper drainage. Our team understands local soil conditions and knows how to design septic systems and house pads that work with (not against) East Texas dirt. Poor drainage planning leads to failed septic systems and flooded building pads - we prevent those problems.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation quotes for all our services. We'll visit your property, assess the scope of work, and provide a detailed written quote. You'll know exactly what you're getting and what it costs before we start. Average response time for quote requests is 24 hours.
